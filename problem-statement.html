ROOKIE HACKERS — BigBasket Challenge

Hackathon Problem Statement (Omega 5.0)

AI-powered Smart Cart Recommendations & Smart Reorder / Subscriptions to reduce missed orders and raise AOV.

⬅ Go Back to Home

1) Problem — No Related Recommendations in Cart

What’s happening?

  • Cart doesn’t guide users to useful add-ons or complements.
  • Missed cross-sell opportunities → lower basket value.
  • Users forget essentials, leading to fragmented or incomplete orders.

Our Feature

  • Smart Cart Recommendation Engine (e.g., bread → butter/jam; rice → dal/oil).
  • Contextual prompts: You might need..., Complete your meal with...
  • Personalization via AI/ML: past purchases, region, trending items.
  • Bundle deals: buy-together discounts (e.g., bread + butter → 10% off).

Impact

  • Higher AOV and better product discovery.
  • Smoother, more complete shopping journey → higher satisfaction & loyalty.

2) Problem — No Weekly/Monthly Reordering for Essentials

What’s happening?

  • Users manually remember to re-add staples like milk, rice, atta, fruits.
  • Stockouts → missed orders & frustration.
  • Risk of churn to competitors with subscriptions.

Our Feature

  • Smart Reorder & Subscription System tracks purchase cycles.
  • Proactive suggestions: weekly/monthly nudges & auto-scheduling.
  • One-click Reorder for past essentials.
  • Subscription perks: steady discounts for repeat deliveries.

Impact

  • Fewer stockouts; on-time replenishment.
  • Predictable recurring revenue and stronger loyalty.

Solution Snapshot

Tech Outline: rules + ML for real-time cart signals, collaborative filtering for complements, purchase-frequency modeling for reorder predictions, and a simple UX layer for prompts, bundles, and subscriptions.
